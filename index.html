Everything agencies, nurses, and parents need — in one pool

Designed for the reality of pediatric GAPP operations: credentialing, coverage, compliance, and family trust.

Shared nurse pool

Agencies mark nurses as private or shareable. Partner agencies can discover, request, and fill coverage from a trusted network.

Credential tracking

GA RN/LPN license, CPR, PALS, TB, GAPP orientation, background checks — all tracked with expiration alerts and upload history.

Case marketplace

Post open GAPP cases with required skills, county, shift type, and auth status. Matching engine ranks nurses by fit.

Parent meet & greets

Families review shortlisted nurses, book virtual or in-person intros, and approve fit — so care starts on day one with trust.

Compliance alerts

Every document, every expiration, every gap — surfaced before it becomes a placement blocker. Bulk renewal reminders.

Audit & reporting

Fill rate, time-to-fill, cross-agency placements, credential gaps. Full activity log for compliance and payer audits.

Secured by Cryptiq · biometric infrastructure for healthcare

Every decision that moves a nurse into a child's home is biometrically signed.

The Nurse Exchange is biometric-gated end-to-end. Cryptiq's 2D liveness, 1:N face match, document verification, and LookAway privacy guard are woven into every role — parent, nurse, agency, admin. Identity isn't assumed. It's proven, signed, and audit-chained.

2D Liveness

Every login, every approval. Camera-based liveness defeats photo, video, and deepfake attacks.

Document + 1:N match

Nurse enrollment scans license, OCRs the data, and face-matches against the document photo before anyone enters the pool.

Signed approvals

Case posts, shortlists, placements, shift acceptances — each produces a hashed biometric signature logged to the audit chain.

LookAway privacy

Sensitive patient data stays hidden unless the right person is looking. Presence-gated content, auto-blur on focus loss.
